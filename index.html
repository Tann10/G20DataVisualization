Welcome to G20 website

Here you will find Gross Domestic Product and expenditure information for G20 countries. The data is retrived from World data website Currently, the expenditure data is available for healthcare, military and education for 6 years (2014-2019). Missing data has been using linear interpolation. The purpose of this website is to help researchers, students, analysts or anyone who is in search of the information. The visualization created are very information and usefuly for driving insights. Feel free to explore further by clicking on the sections below or using navigation links.
