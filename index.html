Welcome to G20 website

Here you will find Gross Domestic Product and Expenditure Information for G20 countries. The members of the G20 are: Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Italy, Japan, Republic of Korea, Mexico, Russia, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, Turkey, United Kingdom, United States, and the European Union. The data is retrieved from the World Data website Currently, the expenditure data is available for health, military, and education for 6 years (2014-2019). Missing data has been treated using linear interpolation. The purpose of this website is to help researchers, students, analysts, or anyone who is in search of G20 expenditure information to visualize the data. The visualization created is very informative, easy to use, and helpful to derive interesting insights. Feel free to explore further by clicking on the sections below or using navigation links.
